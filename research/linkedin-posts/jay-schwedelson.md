**MY TAKE:**       

chwedelson’s “Reply YES” or “Reply GUIDE” strategy maps directly to automation. In GHL or n8n, I’d trigger a workflow that watches for the reply, parses the keyword, tags the contact, and automatically sends the appropriate follow-up. I’d also use those replies as engagement signals for segmentation, since they’re a much stronger indicator of intent than an open or click. That also connects with a pattern I’ve seen across other experts: McGarry focuses on measuring revenue by acquisition source, while Geisler emphasizes behavior-based automation. Replies fit naturally into that same engagement-driven approach, making them valuable for both personalization and list management.
