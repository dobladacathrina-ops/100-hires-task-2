**MY TAKE:** Poyar presents the onboarding survey, automated email flows, and welcome email as separate tactics. I think they’re more effective when treated as one connected onboarding experience. The survey shouldn’t just collect information; it should shape everything that comes next. A subscriber’s responses can determine which welcome sequence they receive, making the onboarding feel more relevant from the first email instead of sending everyone through the same generic flow. Personalization starts with using the information people willingly give you, not just collecting it.
